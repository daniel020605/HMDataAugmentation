background-position样式动画
通过改变background-position属性（第一个值为X轴的位置，第二个值为Y轴的位置）移动背景图片位置，若背景图位置超出组件则超出部分的背景图不显示。
<!-- xxx.hml -->\n<div class=\"container\">\n  <div class=\"content\"></div>\n  <div class=\"content1\"></div>\n</div>
/* xxx.css */\n.container {\n  height: 100%;\n  background-color:#F1F3F5;\n  display: flex;\n  flex-direction: column;\n  justify-content: center;\n  align-items: center;\n  width: 100%;\n}\n.content{\n  width: 400px;\n  height: 400px;\n  /* 不建议图片长宽比为1:1 */\n  background-image: url('common/images/bg-tv.jpg');\n  background-size: 100%;\n  background-repeat: no-repeat;\n  animation: change 3s infinite;\n  border: 1px solid black;\n}\n.content1{\n  margin-top:50px;\n  width: 400px;\n  height: 400px;\n  background-image: url('common/images/bg-tv.jpg');\n  background-size: 50%;\n  background-repeat: no-repeat;\n  animation: change1 5s infinite;\n  border: 1px solid black;\n}\n/* 背景图片移动出组件 */\n@keyframes change{\n  0%{\n    background-position:0px top;\n  }\n  25%{\n    background-position:400px top;\n  }\n  50%{\n    background-position:0px top;\n  }\n  75%{\n    background-position:0px bottom;\n  }\n  100%{\n    background-position:0px top;\n  }\n}\n/* 背景图片在组件内移动 */\n@keyframes change1{\n  0%{\n    background-position:left top;\n  }\n  25%{\n    background-position:50% 50%;\n  }\n  50%{\n    background-position:right bottom;\n  }\n  100%{\n    background-position:left top;;\n  }\n}
background-position仅支持背景图片的移动，不支持背景颜色（background-color）。
